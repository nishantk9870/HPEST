Mites

Mites are one of the smallest arthropods found all over the world. They belong to the class Arachnida and are closely related to ticks and spiders. The majority of mites are ectoparasites of other animals; however, some species are known to infest stored food products, while others are plant feeders. Tens of thousands of different types of mites exist worldwide. Though many mite species are beneficial, others can cause skin irritation or affect allergies.
