Stink Bugs

Stink bugs are a group of true bugs that belong to the order Hemiptera and family Pentatomidae. They are referred to as stink bugs because they product a “stinky” odour when crushed or disturbed. There are over 260 species, most of which are harmless. However, the brown marmorated stink bug (BMSB), or halyomorpha halys is an invasive species that is indigenous to Asia.
